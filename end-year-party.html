End Year Party 2021: Toast To A Successful Year.

  • 2 January 2022
  • CEC
  • News

Our employees are our most valuable resource, and we strive to provide a comfortable working environment while maintaining their dignity. We value our team in a variety of ways. The 2021 end-of-year party at the Amara Hotel in Lavington was one of the many ways we showed our gratitude and appreciation. After a long year of upheaval and diligence, we must recognize the people who work tirelessly to build the company.

Even with the tassels of Covid 19, we had to toast to a year of hard work and accomplishments together in 2021. Because we are a diverse company with established subsidiaries in various industries, we came together to celebrate and bond while looking forward to a prosperous future.
