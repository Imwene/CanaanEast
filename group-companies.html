Our Portfoilio.

Having been established to become a market leader, CanaanEast Company’s subsidiaries have grown and evolved over the years to be able to meet the growing demands of its clients. For more than 9 years, CanaanEast Company’s subsidiaries have been manufacturing, designing, supplying, and providing services in various business verticals. Quality and Innovation are for us two inseparable drivers of our business dynamics. The development of our subsidiaries is based on who we are: a business driven by values of respect and commitment to serve and fuelled by a strong entrepreneurial and dedicated spirit.

Canaan Plastics Ltd was established in 2013 by the Canaaneast Company to manufacture PET Preforms and Bottles used in the packaging of mainly mineral water and juices.

Canaan Developers Limited

At Canaan Developers, we pride ourselves in delivering timely, quality and world class projects while bringing exceptional returns to our stakeholders.

Part of the renowned Louvre Hotels Group, Royal Tulip Canaan Nairobi offers notable facilities such as a Spa & Health Club, Heated swimming pool, Conferencing facilities & a plethora of food & beverage options to suit every palate.

Canaan Factories

Canaan South Limited, is a fruit beverage and drinking water production company based in Luanda, Angola. The fruit beverage was initiated with the launch of the Canajoy juice brand, expanding rapidly to the Production of 4 flavours and mineral water brand, Aura in 2020.
